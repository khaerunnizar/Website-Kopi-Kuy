Gak Ngopi Gak Kuy

Founded in 2017, Kopi Kuy is a retail coffee startup that wants to serve high-quality coffee to its customers. The name Kuy is taken from "yuk" which is our hope to invite everyone who's involved in our business to enjoy the best quality coffee that has ever been served.

We collect our coffee from selected farmers, who plant the coffee on the best land so that it can produce high-quality coffee beans. Then it perfectly processed, taken taken care of and prepared with heart by our best baristas, who are passionate about serving every single glass of happiness for you.

Our Team

We put our hearts into preparing the best coffee for you. We invest in taste, from bean selection, the drying process, and the roasting method to internationally certified baristas with years of experience serving the best coffee.
